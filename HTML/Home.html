About us V

Who are we?

EcoAid is a non-profit organisation dedicated to the making learning about climate change easier.

Using tools similar to any other online learning app, we want people to be able to track their own progress and see what it is that they can do to help in the fight against climate change. With online quizzes, a live curated news feed, and more, EcoAid aims to be a fun alternative way to learn about climate change.

Developed initally as website, we hope to expand into mobile apps and sites so your progress can be right in your pocket.
